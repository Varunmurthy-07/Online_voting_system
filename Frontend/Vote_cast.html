Before you cast you vote!

Voting in India’s General Elections is not a choice—it is a constitutional responsibility. Your vote decides who governs the nation, controls public money, frames laws, and safeguards (or weakens) democracy. An informed voter is powerful; an ignorant voter is dangerous to the republic. Before stepping into the polling booth, be clear, alert, and uncompromising.


Voting is based on universal adult franchise for all citizens aged 18 years and above.
