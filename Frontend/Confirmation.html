Want to help the nation?

Here are a few things you can do as a responsible citizen:

  • Share accurate, unbiased information about civic rights, duties, and public issues.
  • Motivate first-time voters through guidance.
  • Support community initiatives that inform citizens.

Following these points leads to informed participation, fair elections, and stronger public trust. Together, this strengthens democracy and supports the nation’s long-term stability and progress.
